Introduction of Association

Purpose of Association

Name: JAPAN LIGHT VALLEYBALL ASSOCIATION, set up in the beginning of 2019.

The association is led by professional coaches, actively organizes valleyball activities, and carefully teaches valleyball skills.

The association is committed to popularizing the development of valleyball in Japan and promoting a happy exercise for all.

The association is cherished by people of all ages, and the number of active people is large. Handsome men and beautiful women of all nationalities gather, and the number of active members has reached 50.

Introduction of coach

President and head coach of Japan Light Volleyball Association, Chinese national second-level referee.

Born in Dalian, China, she studied at the Liaoning Sports School in his childhood.

Selected as a youth team in Liaoning Province and won the national championship.

At the age of 16, she studied Beijing Sport University, the highest institution of Chinese sports.

Represented the Beijing Sport University in the professional league and achieved excellent results.

After moving to Japan, she is active in the regional volleyball community with coaches, players and referees, and rewarded by KAWAGUCHI.

Now,she is enthusiastic about the development of volleyball in Japan.




Introduction of Light Valleybally

Light Valleybally

The valleyballyl is a sport that integrates sports, leisure and entertainment. As a new sports project, it has been favored by more and more friends. The method of playing and scoring is basically the same as that of competitive volleyball.

　Lighter & Softer

The circumference is slightly larger than the volleyball, and the weight is light, about 120 grams.

The ball is extremely flexible, soft and comfortable to touch, and is not easy to hurt.

　More popular

Because of the small size and the softness of the ball, even you don’t have physical qualities of the professional athletes, but still can feel the infinite charm of the sport.

　More fun

It is a strong collective, suitable for both men and women, and can compete in the same field, work together, unite and forge ahead, and enjoy it.
